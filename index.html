Shun-Qing Zhang

I am a theoretical physicist working on analytic structures in quantum field theory: amplitudes, loop integrals, Wilson loops, correlators, and the geometric ideas that make hard calculations tractable. I am a co-developer of the numerical integration program Trillo.

Research

Geometry and computation for high-energy theory

Feynman integrals

Tropical Monte Carlo methods, Landau analysis, symbol bootstrap, and complete function spaces for multi-loop scattering calculations.

Amplitudes and Wilson loops

Planar five-particle amplitudes, pentagonal Wilson loops with Lagrangian insertion, and pure Yang-Mills amplitude structures.

Positive geometry

Amplituhedra, negative geometries, Grassmannian methods, and ABJM amplitudes in three dimensions.

AdS/CFT correlators

Integrated correlators, Mellin amplitudes, periods, and gravity multiplet correlators in AdS spaces.
